Harry Lewis

MSc Strength & Conditioning Specialist

I have been coaching for 8 years — working across gym environments, university sport, and high performance settings before bringing everything I have learned into one place.

During my MSc in Strength & Conditioning I completed placements with England Golf and volunteered with the UK Sports Institute, alongside five further placements across different sport teams at university level. I also presented at the BASES Physiology Conference.

I now run small group sessions inside Elite Physical Medicine in Aylesbury. The clinical setting is deliberate — my clients are people who take their health seriously, and they deserve a coach who does the same. Every programme is built individually, coached attentively, and updated as you progress.

This is not a gym class. Maximum 6 people per session. Your own plan. Proper coaching.
